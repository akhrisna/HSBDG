COMMUNITY SHARE COLLABORATION

HSBDG is a community for sharing and collaboration located in Bandung, Indonesia. Our members comes from different backgrounds and works in different fields ranging from IT, Journalism and Architecture. If you have ideas or events that you'd like us to help you with or maybe interested in joining us feel free to drop by at our place at Co&Co Space, Dipatiukur 5 Bandung.
